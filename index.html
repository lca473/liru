The **Gloss** column gives the English meanings in the same order as the forms, separated by ` / `. Each form typically receives one concise gloss; forms with two legitimately distinct senses use a parenthetical (e.g., "to get wet (or make wet)"). Regular noun inflections (plural *-ea*, possessive *-eo*, plural possessive *-eoa*) are not listed; see Grammar.md. Agent forms (*-pue*) and repetition forms (*we-*) are included only when they have been explicitly coined.
